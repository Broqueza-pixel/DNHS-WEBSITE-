About Our School

The DELA PAZ NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL is a public school which is located at Almeda Subdivision Brgy. Dela Paz, Binan City Laguna. This is a secondary school that will caters almost one thousand three hundred Eighty-eight total students for this SY 2024-2025. The main objectives of the school is to achieved total students development in all aspects and to prepare students life by offering technologically updated subjects. In line with this, the school has a total of forty-six teachers, two non-teaching staff and headed by a Teacher-In-Charge that consistently dedicated their lives to the profession. These school personnel are well-equipped with appropriate knowledge, skills and enthusiasm that is necessary to the needs of the learners especially during this pandemic situation.

Our Specialized Labs

Computer Lab

Our modern computer lab is equipped with the latest hardware and software to provide students with hands-on experience in programming, graphic design, and digital literacy. Each workstation is designed for collaborative learning and individual exploration.

Science Lab

The science lab provides a safe and stimulating environment for students to conduct experiments and develop their understanding of biology, chemistry, and physics. Well-stocked with equipment and materials, it allows for both guided and independent inquiry.

Math Lab

Our specialized math lab is designed to make mathematical concepts tangible and engaging. With manipulatives, interactive tools, and technology, students can visualize abstract concepts and develop strong problem-solving skills.

School Office

Our administrative office is where students and parents can get information, submit requirements, and coordinate with school staff. The office is equipped with modern facilities to efficiently serve the school community.

Computer Laboratory 2

Our second computer lab is equipped with updated hardware and software to support advanced digital learning. This space provides students with additional resources for technology-based projects, computer programming, and digital skills development.

School Theater

Our theater serves as a venue for performing arts, school assemblies, and special events. With professional lighting, sound systems, and seating, this space allows students to showcase their talents and creativity through drama, music, and dance performances.

Student Organizations

Supreme Secondary Learner Government (SSLG)

Supreme Secondary Learner Government, which refers to a student government or leadership body in a secondary school (high school). They are the highest position among the other organizations. SSLG is a student-led organization that represents the interests of the positivity of the school environment, organizes events, and promotes school spirit.

Youth for Environment in Schools Organization (Yes-O)

Youth for Environment in Schools Organization, they are the one who is in charge when it comes to keeping the cleanliness of the environment of the school. This organization promotes environmental awareness, education, and action among young people in schools.

Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM)

Disaster Risk Reduction and Management, they are the one who is responsible for keeping and teaching the students safeness when it comes to the risks of the disaster. They refer to the systematic process of identifying, assessing, and mitigating the risks of disasters, as well as preparing for, responding to, and recovering from their impacts.

Guidance Peer Advocate (GPA)

Guidance Peer Advocate is an organization that provides support and guidance to students, often through peer mentoring or counseling, to help them navigate academic, personal, and social challenges.

Folkdance Club

The Folkdance Club preserves and promotes traditional cultural dances. Members learn various folk dances that represent the rich cultural heritage of our country and perform at school events, cultural festivals, and community gatherings. This organization helps students appreciate cultural diversity while developing discipline and teamwork.

Majorette Team

Our Majorette Team combines dance, baton twirling, and choreographed routines to create spectacular performances. Team members develop skills in coordination, rhythm, and performance arts while representing the school at various events including parades, sports competitions, and school celebrations.
